    The Wellness Program

    Wellness simply means blossoming in life in all dimensions - being happy, at ease with yourself and with those around you.

    The Wellness Program is a perfect balance of Yoga, Ayurveda and Knowledge. The weekend-long program nudges your attitude from disease to health. It is designed to take you through various effective and practical wellness practices that invite health, wealth and happiness into your life.

    Program Content:

    Ayurveda

    Simple and practical aspects of Ayurveda, (translated as the science of life), are taught in the program. The timeless knowledge of this ancient Indian science prescribes cures as well as ways to stay healthy. The knowledge of this opens up new dimensions to live life naturally and with good health.

    Education

    Awareness through scientific education about different aspects of our life is the third emphasis the effects of food, stress and exercise on our body and mind and ways to remedy the same.

    Yoga

    Yoga means balancing or harmonizing all aspects of our existence the gross and the subtle body, and the mind. Yoga is not merely a set of exercises, it is an attitude that leads to balance. Asanas, pranayamas (breathing exercises) and meditation are an essential part of it.

    Our Methodology

    Our wellness solution are most relevant in todays context of a generally stressful lifestyle, demands at workplace, demands at a personal and societal levels.

    yurveda + Yoga + Knowledge

    yurveda

    Simple and easy-to-follow practical tips that have their origins in yurveda are shared. These regular suggestions help to not only to make a difference in life but also make life different.

    Yoga

    Fitness is the priority. Some basic yogasanas are taught that help the body-mind complex to remain energized and active throughout the day.

    Knowledge

    Life throws up innumerable challenges. That which equips you to face them head on is knowledge. When this knowledge gets deeply embedded in you, it is wisdom. We share our timeless knowledge with you to help you live a happier and fuller life.

      Moola mantras - our underlying philosophy

      i) dharma artha kma mokshnam rogyam moolam uthamam ||
      dharma are the moral values and duties in life and artha, the economic values. k**ma is pleasure or desire which drives life. moksha are the spiritual values that help an individual grow as a human being. These offer an individual a blueprint for life. The right values of dharma are the cornerstones for artha to be meaningful. The prosperity of artha will be needed to support k**ma. The path of spiritual growth culminating in moksha is a beautiful balance of dharma, artha, and k**ma. The seamless interweaving of these four pursuits in life makes life healthy and happy.

      ii) swasthasya swsthya rakshanam
      Protecting the health of the healthy is another core tenet of yurveda. The attitude that the healthy dont need any looking after and can eat and sleep at will, can destroy health in no time. The Wellness Program brings out the health nurturing aspects of yurveda making it very practical yet interesting. yurveda does not work on one size fits all. It is a customized and individualized approach to health.

      iii) sarvam pancha bhautikam
      Everything, living and non-living, is made up of the pancha mhbhoota, the five basic elements; ksha, vyu, agni, jala and prithvi. The universe we live in, the food we ingest and our bodies are all made up of these five elements. The Wellness Program helps to decode these building blocks to give you a deeper understanding of the workings of the body-mind complex. When all these aspects were catered to, WELLNESS - mental, physical, emotional and financial - became an inseparable part of life.
